About your Luthier


John Knauff is the owner and sole luthier at Pink Guitarz, a Denver-based musical instrument repair business he founded in 1986. With over 38 years of experience, John expertly restores guitars and other stringed instruments, ensuring they perform at their best. A lifelong musician, John has been playing guitar since the age of 11. In addition to his work as a luthier, he is also a producer and composer, writing and recording original music as well as scoring for various film projects.

History of Pink Guitarz


Pink Guitarz was founded in 1986 by brothers Mark and John Knauff in Centreville, Virginia. Initially, their vision was to produce a line of electric guitars in various shades of pink, regardless of style. However, they soon recognized that this concept wasn't practical and discovered a larger demand for instrument repair and customization services. Embracing this new direction, Pink Guitarz was born. Over the years, the business relocated multiple times, eventually settling in Denver, Colorado, where it was relaunched by John after Markâ€™s retirement. Today, Pink Guitarz continues to provide fast and efficient repair services for all stringed instruments, building on the legacy of craftsmanship that has defined the company for decades.
